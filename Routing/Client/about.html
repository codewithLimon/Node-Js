About Us

Welcome to Limon, your go-to source for all things fresh and citrusy! We're dedicated to providing you with the very best lemons and other citrus fruits, with a focus on quality, freshness, and customer satisfaction.

Founded in 20XX by Jane Doe, Limon has come a long way from its beginnings in a small backyard orchard. When Jane first started out, her passion for growing organic, pesticide-free citrus fruits drove her to quit her day job and start this business.
